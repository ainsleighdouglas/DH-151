About

This choropleth map illustrates the prevalence of carbon emissions across the world, as compared to units of GDP. Data for all countries was not available, but this map gives a good indication of countries that over-emit based on their economic production. We then compared each country's emissions level to the global average from this dataset, to give an idea of how much more or less countries are emitting compared to what their "fair share" would be.
